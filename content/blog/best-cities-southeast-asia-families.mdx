# Best Cities in Southeast Asia for Worldschooling Families in 2025

Southeast Asia remains one of the most popular regions for worldschooling families — and for good reason. Low cost of living, warm climate, vibrant local culture, and a well-established community of location-independent families make it a natural choice for your first (or tenth) year on the road.

## 1. Chiang Mai, Thailand

Chiang Mai has been the capital of the digital nomad world for over a decade, and families are no different. With a thriving co-working scene, excellent international food, and a relaxed pace of life, it's hard to beat.

- **Average monthly cost (family of 4):** ~$1,500 USD
- **Family score:** 80/100
- **Why families love it:** Active worldschool meetup community, affordable bilingual schools, incredible street food

## 2. Bali (Canggu / Ubud), Indonesia

Bali offers two distinct experiences: Canggu for the surf-and-cowork lifestyle, and Ubud for the spiritual, nature-immersed experience. Both are popular with families.

- **Average monthly cost (family of 4):** ~$1,800 USD
- **Family score:** 75/100
- **Why families love it:** Strong expat community, Waldorf-inspired local schools, stunning natural environment

## 3. Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam

HCMC is Southeast Asia's rising star for families. Affordable, chaotic in the best way, and increasingly well-connected internationally.

- **Average monthly cost (family of 4):** ~$1,400 USD
- **Family score:** 72/100
- **Why families love it:** Excellent Vietnamese food, low cost of living, improving international school options
