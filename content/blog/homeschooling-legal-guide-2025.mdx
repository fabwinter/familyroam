# Is Homeschooling Legal Where You Want to Move? A 2025 Guide

One of the first questions every worldschooling family asks before choosing a destination: *is homeschooling actually legal here?*

The answer varies enormously by country — from full legal recognition to complete prohibition. Here's what you need to know.

## Fully Legal (with minimal registration)

- **Portugal** — Legal with curriculum flexibility. Popular with expat families.
- **Thailand** — Legal and growing. Registration required.
- **Mexico** — Legal, registration varies by state.
- **Colombia** — Legal with some administrative requirements.

## Legal but Complex

- **Germany** — Effectively illegal due to *Schulpflicht* (compulsory school attendance). A notable exception for expats.
- **Sweden** — Heavily restricted since 2011.
- **Brazil** — Technically legal after a 2019 Supreme Court ruling, but implementation is patchy.

## Generally Permissive for Expats

Many countries that have restrictive homeschooling laws for citizens are more relaxed about enforcement for short-stay foreign families. Always verify with a local education lawyer before making long-term plans.
